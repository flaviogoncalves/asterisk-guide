# Part I — Foundations {.unnumbered}

Part I takes you from nothing to a working phone system. You will meet Asterisk and
the Sangoma ecosystem that surrounds it, install Asterisk 22 from source (and in a
container), and build your first PBX on the modern PJSIP channel — one that registers
real phones and places and receives calls.

By the end of this part you will have a running system on your bench and the
vocabulary you need to make sense of everything that follows: channels, the dialplan,
endpoints, and the console you will live in for the rest of the book.
