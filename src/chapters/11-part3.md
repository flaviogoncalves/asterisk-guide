# Part III — Dialplan & Call Features {.unnumbered}

Part III is where Asterisk stops being a SIP switch and becomes a phone system. The
dialplan is the heart of that transformation: the logic that decides what happens to
every call. We cover it from the fundamentals through the advanced patterns — pattern
matching, subroutines, time conditions, and database lookups.

With the dialplan in hand we add the features users actually expect from a PBX —
transfer, parking, pickup, conferencing with ConfBridge, and voicemail — and finish
with call queues for a basic contact center.
