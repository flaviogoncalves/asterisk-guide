# Part IV — Integration & Operations {.unnumbered}

Part IV takes your system from "it works on the bench" to "it runs in production."
You will account for every call with CDR and CEL, then drive Asterisk from the outside
using its three integration interfaces — AMI, AGI, and ARI — to connect it to your own
applications.

Finally we make it operable and safe: storing configuration in a database with the
Realtime architecture, hardening the system against the constant background of VoIP
attacks, and deploying, monitoring, and scaling it for real-world use.
