# Asterisk Guide — Sponsorship

*Asterisk Guide* (2nd edition, **Asterisk 22 LTS**, PJSIP-first, lab-verified) is given away
as a free **EPUB and PDF**, with the open Markdown source and a reproducible Docker lab on
GitHub. Sponsorship funds that free distribution and places your brand in front of working
Asterisk developers, system integrators, and ITSPs worldwide.

## Where sponsors appear

In the **free EPUB and PDF** only — the paid Amazon/KDP paperback stays ad-free:

- A **Sponsors page** in the front matter (founding sponsor + lab sponsors).
- A one-line **sponsor credit at the opening of each chapter** (the founding sponsor on
  general chapters; the lab's sponsor on chapters with a sponsored lab).

## 2. Lab / chapter sponsorship

Sponsor a specific hands-on lab so readers complete it against your service. Your credit
appears **at the opening of that chapter** and in the **Lab sponsors** section of the
Sponsors page.

| Item | Price (USD/edition) | Placement |
|------|--------------------:|-----------|
| **Lab sponsor** (e.g. the SIP Trunking & DID labs) | 800 | Sponsor credit at the chapter opening + Lab-sponsors listing (name, blurb, link). |

A new edition ships roughly **yearly** with each Asterisk LTS line. Annual or multi-edition
commitments are available at a discount. Non-cash sponsorship (hardware, lab hosting,
translation funding) can be negotiated.
