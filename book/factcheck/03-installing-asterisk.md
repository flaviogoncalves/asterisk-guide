## Summary

- **Verified:** 25 (incl. #4, #20, #29 resolved below)
- **Wrong (fixed):** 6 — #6 (dahdi_dummy clock source), #15 & #16 (codec_opus "bundled/free"), #18 & #23 (make config installs systemd unit). Quiz Q6 (line 678) was also corrected to match the dahdi_dummy fix; its answer (ConfBridge + Music on Hold) remains valid.
- **Unverified:** 0 — all three resolved:
  - #4 (line 23) iLBC 18 MIPS — sourced to Global IP Sound (TI C54x: ~18 MIPS/ch @30 ms, ~15 MIPS/ch @20 ms). Claim revised to anchor the platform and frame sizes precisely (iLBC White Paper / GIPS technical material; not in RFC 3951).
  - #20 (line 221) "Add Default Sources" — no such option; rewritten to the real menus *Core Sound Packages* / *Music On Hold File Packages* / *Extras Sound Packages*, downloaded at `make install` (docs.asterisk.org Using-Menuselect; confirmed absent from menuselect source).
  - #29 (line 67) Ubuntu ISO 404 — replaced un-suffixed filename with the releases.ubuntu.com/24.04/ directory (HTTP 200) plus a current point-release example (`ubuntu-24.04.4-live-server-amd64.iso`, HTTP 200).
